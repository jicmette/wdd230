Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to represent the interests of every business in Saltillo, Coah. Mex., strengthen the commerce sector, to promote growth and create a space where people know about the local offer of services and distribution of work.

Every business will know about the benefits of joining a community where they will receive capacitation, and legal support, resources and more.
