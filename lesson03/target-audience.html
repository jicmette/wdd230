Target Audience

Every person with access to the internet, but more focused in people with micro, small, medium and large-sized companies with the desire of capacitation, guide with legal and financial topics to develop a healthy system of work in their business.

Persona 1

Restaurant Owner - Chef

Fictional Name

Mary Jane

Job title/major responsibilities:

Owner - Chef

Demographics

  • Age: 36 years old
  • Education: Bachelor Degree in Gastronomy
  • Ethnicity: Latina
  • Family Status: Married with two children

Goals and tasks

She is recruiting people to join to her team, and searching for a qualified kitchen assitant. She wants to give an excelent food service.

  • Focused in recruiting an excelent team (waiters, kitchen assistant, manager and manager assistant, etc).
  • Confirming two partners to inject money to the project
  • Searching for recognize food providers that helps her to have quality and economic supplies.

Enviroment

She has a computer with Office 365 to manage any administration task. She has a great IT consultant that helps her to be ready with any issue. She is always using her email and WhatsApp to stay connected with people that contribute to her business.

Quote

"A customer is the most important visitor on our premises, he is not dependent on us. We are dependent on him."

Scenarios

  • How can I plan the growth of my restaurant?
  • Where do I obtain the licenses to operate as a restaurant?
