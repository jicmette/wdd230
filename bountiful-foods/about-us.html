About Us

Our History

Bountiful Foods was founded in 1972 by two brothers, John, and James Smith. The brothers were passionate about healthy eating, and they wanted to create a company that would make it easy for people to get their hands on fresh, local fruits.

The company started out small, with just a few employees and a small storefront in Carlsbad, California. But Bountiful Foods quickly grew in popularity, and within a few years, it had expanded to several other cities across the country, they have many food trucks that cover the coastal area from Los Angeles to San Diego.

The company's success is due in large part to its commitment to quality. Bountiful Foods sources its fruits from local farmers, and it only uses the freshest, most ripe fruits. The company also has a strict quality control process, which ensures that all its fruits are of the highest quality.

In addition to its commitment to quality, Bountiful Foods is also committed to sustainability. The company uses recyclable packaging, and it offsets its carbon emissions. Bountiful Foods is also a member of the Sustainable Agriculture Coalition, and it works to promote sustainable farming practices.

Today, Bountiful Foods is one of the leading fruit companies in the United States. The company has over 100 stores across the country, and it employs over 1,000 people. Bountiful Foods is committed to providing its customers with the freshest, most delicious fruits, and it is also committed to sustainability.
